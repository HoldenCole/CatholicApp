Morning
Mane
Morning Offering
Upon waking, immediately offer your day to God. "Lord, I offer Thee all my prayers, works, joys, and sufferings of this day."
Attend Holy Mass
Padre Pio's Mass was the center of his life, often lasting two hours. Attend Mass daily if possible.
Mental Prayer (30 minutes)
Spend thirty minutes in silent mental prayer, meditating on the Passion of Christ.
·
Throughout the Day
Per Diem
Pray the Full Rosary
Padre Pio called it "the weapon." Pray at least five decades daily.
Spiritual Reading (15 minutes)
Read from The Imitation of Christ, the lives of the saints, or St. John of the Cross.
Visit to the Blessed Sacrament
Sit or kneel before Jesus in the tabernacle in silence.
Pray the Angelus
Pray the Angelus at noon (or the Regina Caeli during Eastertide).
·
Evening
Vespere
Examination of Conscience
Before sleep, examine your conscience on the day's sins and failings.

Unite every pain or difficulty to Christ's Passion.
