A4 Monogram — Symmetrical Variations

Five ways to make the AD monogram bilaterally symmetrical on the vertical axis.

A4a — Stacked Vertical

Large upright "A" in red over a smaller italic "D" in gold, both perfectly centered. Cross above, hairlines framing. Clean vertical symmetry — like a missal spine.
