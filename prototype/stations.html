Statio I  ·  XIV
Statio Prima
Jesus Is Condemned to Death
Iesus ad mortem condemnatur
✠
℣. Adorámus te, Christe, et benedícimus tibi.
℟. Quia per sanctam Crucem tuam redemísti mundum.
Consider how Jesus, after having been scourged and crowned with thorns, was unjustly condemned by Pilate to die on the Cross.
Oratio  ·  Prayer

Pater Noster… Ave Maria… Gloria Patri…

Stabat Mater dolorosa, iuxta Crucem lacrimosa, dum pendebat Filius.
