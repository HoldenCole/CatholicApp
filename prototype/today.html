℟.   Feria Sexta
Abstinence from flesh-meat
All the faithful who have reached the age of reason abstain from the flesh of warm-blooded animals, in memory of the Passion of Our Lord.
Indulgentia Plenária
Today the Prayer Before a Crucifix may be prayed for a plenary indulgence (Lenten Friday after Communion).
