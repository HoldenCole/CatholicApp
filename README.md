# Introibo

**A prayer companion for traditional Catholics attending the Latin Mass.**

*Introibo ad altare Dei. Ad Deum qui laetificat juventutem meam.*

No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Works completely offline. All data stored locally on device.

---

## Overview

Introibo puts the complete 1962 Missale Romanum in your pocket with daily Propers automatically interleaved with the Ordinary in correct liturgical order, so you can follow along at Mass from the Prayers at the Foot of the Altar through the Last Gospel. Beyond the Missal, it is a full daily companion with the Divine Office, an interactive bead-by-bead Rosary, Stations of the Cross, 22 traditional prayers, a Latin school, and a reference library, all in side-by-side Latin and English.

Built for Catholics who attend the Traditional Latin Mass at FSSP, ICKSP, SSPX, and diocesan TLM parishes.

## Features

### Missal

- **120+ daily Propers** (Introit, Collect, Epistle, Gradual, Gospel, Offertory, Secret, Communion, Postcommunion) imported from the [DivinumOfficium](https://github.com/DivinumOfficium/divinum-officium) open-source database
- **Calendar engine** resolving the correct proper for any date via Temporale (Easter-relative moveable feasts covering every Sunday and major weekday) and Sanctorale (18 fixed-date feasts including Christmas, Epiphany, Assumption, Immaculate Conception)
- **Interleaved Mass view** weaving Ordinary and Propers together automatically in correct liturgical order: Preces, Introit, Kyrie, Gloria, Collect, Epistle, Gradual, Gospel, Credo, Offertory, Secret, Sanctus, Canon, Communion, Postcommunion, Last Gospel
- Proper sections visually distinguished with a red left border
- Toolbar displays today's feast name
- Complete Ordinary of the 1962 Missale Romanum (13 sections)
- Side-by-side Latin and English via BilingualLine component
- Support for 1962, 1955, and pre-1955 Missal rubrics

### Today Screen

- **Propers of the Day card** showing today's proper title with Epistle and Gospel scripture references, tap to read the full readings without scrolling through the Missal
- Dark walnut header with liturgical colour pip and seasonal context
- Daily psalm verse from the Vulgate with Douay-Rheims translation (30 curated verses, rotating by day of year)
- Penance card with three layers: obligatory fasting and abstinence, saint-specific penance, and 8 selectable optional voluntary penances
- Seasonal countdowns for Advent, Lent, and Easter
- First Friday, First Saturday, and Ember Day flags
- Devotion links: Divine Office, Stations of the Cross, Examination of Conscience, Morning Offering
- Rosary card with today's mystery set and last-prayed date
- Saint card with follow status and daily streak
- Schola card showing lesson mastery progress

### Prayers

- **22 traditional prayers** in Latin and English with decorative drop cap on the first line
- **Time-aware featured prayer**: Morning Offering (before noon), Salve Regina (noon to 6pm), Suscipe (after 6pm)
- **Sort and filter dropdown**: By Type (grouped by liturgical category) or A to Z (alphabetical)
- Categories: Rosarium, Missa, Devotiones, Ante Crucifixum
- All prayers respect the language mode setting (Latin only, English only, or both side-by-side)
- Prayers include: Sign of the Cross, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer, Salve Regina, Apostles' Creed, Confiteor, Kyrie, Gloria in Excelsis, Sanctus, Agnus Dei, Domine Non Sum Dignus, Morning Offering, Memorare, Act of Contrition, Anima Christi, Prayer Before a Crucifix, Tantum Ergo, St. Michael Prayer, Adoro Te Devote, Suscipe

### Rosary

- **Interactive bead-by-bead flow** through 79 prayer steps
- Three traditional mystery sets: Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious (plus Seasonal rotation)
- **Mystery title, English name, and fruit displayed persistently** throughout each decade in a highlighted band
- Daily mystery rotation following traditional custom with seasonal overrides (Joyful in Advent, Sorrowful in Lent)
- Opening prayers (Sign of Cross, Creed, Our Father, 3 Hail Marys for Faith Hope Charity, Glory Be) through 5 decades (Mystery announcement, meditation, Our Father, 10 Hail Marys, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer) to closing (Salve Regina, Sign of Cross)
- Decade progress dots and bead-in-decade dots
- Completion tracking with rosary-prayed date
- No Luminous Mysteries, faithful to pre-2002 tradition

### Stations of the Cross

- **Winding pilgrimage path** landing page with alternating left and right station cards
- **Color-coded mood markers**: red (death scenes), gold (Marian encounters), gray (tomb)
- Rounded card backgrounds with curved dashed connectors between stations
- Dark walnut header with cross and XIV Stationes
- 14 individual stations with: large Roman numeral, title (English and Latin), meditation text, Adoramus Te versicle and response with English translations, Pater Ave Gloria prompt, and Stabat Mater stanza
- Begin the Way button and direct station access
- Full navigation with back and next between stations

### Divine Office

- **All 8 canonical hours** of the 1962 Roman Breviary: Matutinum, Laudes, Prima, Tertia, Sexta, Nona, Vesperae, Completorium
- **Canonical clock dial** with hours positioned at their traditional times; current hour glows
- Side-by-side Latin and English rendering for all part types: versicles and responses, hymns, antiphons, psalms with individual verses, capitula, canticles, collects, responsories, confiteor, and Marian antiphons (Alma Redemptoris, Ave Regina, Regina Caeli, Salve Regina by season)
- Intro block with red left accent stripe
- Dark walnut header with hour name, English translation, and traditional time

### Confession

- **Examination of Conscience** walking through the Ten Commandments with traditional self-examination questions for each commandment
- **Two guided confession paths** (Liber I and Liber II) with step-by-step numbered instructions, Latin phrases, titles, and body text
- Landing screen with direct access to examen or guided paths
- Deep-link from Today screen devotion section

### Latin School

- **10 progressive lessons** from vowels and pronunciation through reading the Vulgate
- **Interactive flip-card flashcards** showing Latin front, tap to reveal English translation and phonetic pronunciation
- **Multiple-choice quizzes** (5 questions each) testing both Latin-to-English and English-to-Latin, with randomized choices, score tracking, and try-again option
- Lessons with intro text, section types (lesson, tip, cards, phrase, table, summary)
- Mastery tracking per lesson with checkmark seal indicator
- Overall mastery count displayed on Learn tab and Today screen

### Reference Library

- **37 entries across 7 categories** covering Catholic doctrine, sacraments, devotional practice, and tradition
- Bold red section headers with decorative lines
- Detail view with dark walnut header, drop cap summary, History Practice Notes sections, and scripture block with Latin and English
- ScrollView layout with dividers

### Saints

- **7 patron saints** with detailed practice schedules
- Follow or unfollow a saint with daily streak tracking (resets if a day is skipped)
- Quote block, charism description, and three practice periods: Morning, Throughout Day, Evening
- Streak counter with gold dots in header
- Saint-specific penance displayed on Today screen when following

### App Experience

- **Splash screen** on each launch with dark walnut background, animated gold monstrance icon, app title, and Ad altare Dei subtitle
- **Three-page swipeable onboarding** for first-time users: Welcome (monstrance icon and 4 value props), Tradition First (5 commitments), Everything You Need (8 feature rows)
- **Three display themes**: Parchment (warm vellum background), Clean White (modern white with walnut tab bar), Dark (deep walnut for low light), applied instantly across all screens
- **Adjustable text size** via continuous slider (0.8x to 1.4x range, default 1.15x) with live preview
- **Language mode**: Latin only, English only, or side-by-side bilingual, affecting Missal, Prayers, and Divine Office
- **Feedback option** in Settings for reporting issues or suggesting features
- Five-tab navigation: Hodie (Today), Missa (Missal), Oratio (Prayers), Schola (Learn), Liber (Reference)

## Technical Details

### Architecture

- **SwiftUI** with `@Observable`, `@AppStorage`, `@State`, `@Environment` patterns
- **iOS 17.0+** deployment target
- **XcodeGen** (`project.yml`) for Xcode project generation
- Bundle ID: `app.introibo.Introibo`

### Data Layer

- **ContentStore** singleton (`@Observable`) loading 13 bundled JSON files on first access: prayers, reference, saints, courses, missal, mysteries, rosary_prayers, stations, hours, marian_antiphons, examen, confession_guides, propers
- **LiturgicalContext** engine computing full liturgical date info for any date: season, colour, feria, Marian antiphon, Rosary mystery, penance obligations, proper slug
- **Computus** algorithm (Anonymous Gregorian) for Easter date calculation
- **ProperCalendar** resolving any date to a proper slug via Temporale (Easter-relative offsets) and Sanctorale (fixed-date feast table)
- **UserProgress** facade over UserDefaults for saints streaks, rosary history, lesson mastery, all local with no sync

### Design System

- **Palette**: 9 raw colour tokens (parchment, ink, sepia, muted, sanctuaryRed, goldLeaf, walnut, walnutHi, ivory) with 5 semantic theme-aware computed properties (pageBackground, primaryText, secondaryText, tertiaryText, frameLine)
- **Typography**: 10 font scales using system serif stack (Playfair Display, EB Garamond, Cormorant Garamond when bundled), body fonts scaled by user preference via `FontSizeScale`
- **BilingualLine**: reusable component rendering Latin and English text based on language mode, with automatic HTML tag stripping (br to newline, regex for all other tags)
- **SmallLabelStyle**: ubiquitous modifier for uppercase italic tracked labels

### CI/CD

- **GitHub Actions** workflow on `macos-15` runner
- Builds on push to `main` and `claude/**` branches
- Simulator build for compilation verification on all pushes
- **Fastlane** `beta` lane for TestFlight deployment on main-branch pushes
- App Store Connect API key authentication via `-authenticationKeyPath` flags
- Automatic code signing with team ID from secrets

### Content Sources

- Missal Propers imported from [DivinumOfficium](https://github.com/DivinumOfficium/divinum-officium) via `scripts/import_propers.py`
- All other liturgical content authored from the 1962 Missale Romanum, Roman Breviary, and traditional prayer books
- All texts are public domain (pre-copyright liturgical sources)

## Building

### Prerequisites

- macOS with **Xcode 15+** (Xcode 26 recommended)
- **XcodeGen**: `brew install xcodegen`

### Build

```sh
xcodegen generate
open Introibo.xcodeproj
```

Build target: iOS 17.0+. No code signing required for local simulator builds.

### CI Deployment

Push to `main` triggers the GitHub Actions workflow which:
1. Selects the latest available Xcode
2. Generates the Xcode project via XcodeGen
3. Builds for simulator (compilation check)
4. Archives and uploads to TestFlight via Fastlane (main branch only)

Required GitHub Secrets: `ASC_KEY_ID`, `ASC_ISSUER_ID`, `ASC_KEY_P8`, `APPLE_TEAM_ID`
