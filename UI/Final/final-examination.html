Examen Conscientiæ
Commandment II of X 2 of 10
II
Thou shalt not take the name of the Lord thy God in vain.
Non assúmes nomen Dómini Dei tui in vanum.
Have I used God's name carelessly or in anger?
Have I cursed or used profane language?
Have I sworn a false oath or broken a vow made to God?
Have I spoken disrespectfully of sacred things, the saints, or the Church?
Have I murmured against God when things went wrong?
Have I blasphemed or shown contempt for God or holy things?
Have I used God's name in a frivolous or irreverent manner?
Have I failed to fulfill promises made in God's name?
